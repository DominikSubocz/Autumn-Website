About Us

Who are we?

We are just a small group of people running a small website. We love to travel around the world, see how autumn looks like in different places. We love helping people out but especially we love autumn

More about us.

We wanted to create a friendly community where everyone would respect and help each other, but also a place for people to have a chat with each other.

Our Story

It all begun at high school. When we were working in a group and came up with Clarkson Seasons. Firstly starting out as a portfolio website, slowly growing into a place for people to chat and for admiring autumn.
